Project Overview

Three communities, three stories — told by the people who live them. This brand film series was built around a simple premise: that the most powerful way to reach a culture is to hand the narrative back to it.

Working across three Caribbean markets, we embedded with local creators and community figures to develop a series of films that didn't just feature these audiences but were shaped by them. Every visual decision, every voice, every location was chosen from within — not imposed from outside.

The result was a body of work that resonated where brand campaigns typically fall flat: in communities that have seen enough of themselves through someone else's lens.
